Negative Impact

The marvels of technology are undeniable, yet like anything, excessive exposure can become overwhelming. Our constant connectivity poses challenges in disconnecting and finding moments to relax. Additionally, prolonged screen time can result in physical discomforts such as eyestrain, hand-wrist ailments, and disrupted sleep patterns. Unfortunately, spending excessive time in virtual environments also carries significant risks. The rapid dissemination of misinformation, cyberbullying, and other negative online behaviors can exacerbate mental health issues like anxiety, depression and addiction. social media, in particular, can influence perceptions of self-worth and belonging, impacting real-life relationships. It's imperative to approach online content with discernment and avoid accepting everything at face value.
